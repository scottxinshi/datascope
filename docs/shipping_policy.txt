
SHIPPING POLICY

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the country.
Express shipping takes 2-3 business days and costs an additional $15.
International shipping takes 10-14 business days.
Orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping.
Shipping is not available to PO boxes for orders over 10kg.
All orders are insured up to $500 by default.
Tracking information is emailed within 24 hours of dispatch.
